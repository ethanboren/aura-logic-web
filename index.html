About Us

We're just getting started.

Aura Logic was founded on a simple conviction: the gap between information and decision is where value is created — and lost.

We're a small team of engineers and quantitative analysts building the infrastructure to bridge that gap. Our platform is in active development, and we're partnering with early adopters who want to shape the future of financial intelligence.

Technology

Three layers of intelligence.

Our AWS-powered stack processes real-time global data through a sophisticated pipeline — from ingestion to actionable prediction.

High-Frequency Ingestion

Automated capture of global data flows — news APIs, social networks, regulatory filings — processed through optimized AWS infrastructure in real time.

AWS · Kinesis · Lambda

NLP Engine

Advanced language models extract named entities, analyze sentiment, and identify emerging narratives across millions of documents per day.
